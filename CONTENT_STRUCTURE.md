## Content Writing Guidelines

### Voice & Tone:
- Professional but approachable
- Confident expertise without arrogance  
- Local community focus
- Solution-oriented
- Transparent and honest

### Writing Style:
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for easy scanning
- Clear headings and subheadings
- Active voice preferred
- Technical terms explained simply
- Local references and landmarks

### SEO Content Rules:
- Primary keyword in H1 and first 150 words
- Secondary keywords in H2s
- LSI keywords naturally throughout
- Local keywords in every page
- FAQ sections for voice search
- Schema markup for all structured content

### Conversion Elements:
- Multiple CTAs per page (top, middle, bottom)
- Phone numbers clickable on mobile
- Trust signals throughout
- Social proof (reviews, testimonials)
- Urgency/scarcity where appropriate
- Clear value propositions
- Before/after visuals
- Guarantee statements
