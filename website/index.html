Our Professional Air Duct Cleaning Process

A comprehensive 6-step process designed to deliver exceptional results

1

Initial Inspection & Assessment

We conduct a thorough inspection of your HVAC system and ductwork to identify problem areas and develop a customized cleaning plan.

2

System Preparation & Protection

We protect your home with drop cloths and seal off work areas. All vents and registers are carefully removed for thorough cleaning.

3

High-Powered Vacuum Extraction

Using powerful truck-mounted equipment, we create negative pressure to extract dust, debris, and contaminants from your entire duct system.

4

Agitation & Deep Cleaning

Specialized brushes and compressed air tools agitate and dislodge stubborn buildup, ensuring complete removal of contaminants.

5

Sanitization & Treatment

We apply EPA-approved antimicrobial treatments to eliminate bacteria, mold, and other microorganisms, leaving your ducts sanitized.

6

Final Inspection & Restoration

We reinstall all components, conduct a final quality check, and provide before/after photos showing the dramatic improvement.
