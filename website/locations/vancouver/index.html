Pacific Northwest Climate Impact

Vancouver's mild, humid climate creates ideal conditions for dust mites, mold growth, and allergen accumulation in air ducts. Our professional cleaning removes these contaminants, creating healthier indoor air for your family.

Common Vancouver Air Quality Issues:

  • Seasonal pollen from Douglas Fir and other local trees
  • Moisture-related mold and mildew growth
  • Dust mite populations thriving in humid conditions
  • Pet dander accumulation in closed winter months
